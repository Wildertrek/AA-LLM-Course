# Astra Fellowship (Constellation Institute)

## Overview
**Mission:** To accelerate AI safety research and talent by connecting exceptional people with leading mentors and opportunities.
**URL:** [https://constellation.org/programs/astra](https://constellation.org/programs/astra)
**Location:** Berkeley, CA (In-person).
**Duration:** Fully funded 3-6 month fellowship.

## Focus Areas
*   **Technical Research:** Advanced safety engineering and alignment.
*   **Governance & Strategy:** High-level policy and strategic planning.
*   **Field-Building:** Expanding the AI safety ecosystem and talent pipeline.

## Pedagogy: Learning Path for Astra
Astra is designed for those ready to transition into full-time roles. The pedagogy is career-centric.

### Module 1: Domain Expertise
*   **Goal:** Deepen technical or policy knowledge in a niche area.
*   **Action:** Intensive study of specific safety sub-fields like *Scalable Oversight* or *AI Forensic analysis*.
*   **Resource:** Review the [Constellation Research](https://constellation.org/) themes.

### Module 2: Professional Networking
*   **Goal:** Integrate into the Berkeley safety community.
*   **Action:** Attend workshops, seminars, and networking events (in-person if possible).
*   **Strategy:** Engage with researchers from Redwood Research, METR, and Anthropic.

### Module 3: Career Transitioning
*   **Goal:** Prepare for placement in frontier labs.
*   **Action:** Work with Astra's career support to refine your "Theory of Change" (how your work reduces existential risk).
*   **Output:** A portfolio of work ready for job applications at major AI labs.

### Module 4: Incubation (Optional)
*   **Goal:** Launch a new safety organization or project.
*   **Action:** Develop a business/research plan and pitch for Astra's incubation services.
