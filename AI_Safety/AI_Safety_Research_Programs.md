# AI Safety Research & Upskilling Programs

Based on recommendations from the Anthropic Fellows Program, these are the leading organizations and programs for gaining further experience in AI Safety research and alignment.

## 1. [MATS (ML Alignment & Theory Scholars)](MATS_Program.md)
**URL:** [https://www.matsprogram.org/](https://www.matsprogram.org/)
- **Focus:** An educational seminar and independent research program based in Berkeley, CA.
- **Model:** Scholars are paired with established AI safety mentors to work on specific research agendas.
- **Key Areas:** Mechanistic Interpretability, Agent Foundations, Scalable Oversight, and Governance.
- **Outcome:** Designed to pivot high-potential researchers into full-time AI safety careers at labs like Anthropic, OpenAI, or DeepMind.

## 2. [SPAR (Supervised Program for Alignment Research)](SPAR_Program.md)
**URL:** [https://sparai.org/](https://sparai.org/)
- **Focus:** A remote-first, project-based program for people wanting to gain their first experience in alignment research.
- **Model:** Pairs small groups of students/researchers with a mentor to execute a 3-month research project.
- **Key Areas:** Empirical evaluation, red teaming, and sociotechnical impacts.
- **Outcome:** Provides a tangible research artifact (paper or blog post) to strengthen applications for fellowships or PhD programs.

## 3. [Astra (Constellation)](Astra_Fellowship.md)
**URL:** [https://constellation.org/programs/astra](https://constellation.org/programs/astra)
- **Focus:** Managed by Constellation, Astra focuses on fellowships and career development in the broader AI safety ecosystem.
- **Model:** Often acts as a bridge for researchers to enter specialized labs or policy organizations.
- **Key Areas:** Technical safety, AI governance, and field-building.
- **Outcome:** Targeted networking and mentorship within the "Constellation" of safety-focused organizations.
